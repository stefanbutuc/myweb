“Joseph Foundation”

“Joseph Foundation” had as field of activity the protection of child in difficulty. I started working with them in 2003. It was the beginning of economic and social normalization in Romania. I was impressed by the dedication of the staff working there - they devoted themselves, their time, energy and knowledge to the interest of the child in need in the very difficult years after the Revolution of '89.

Here I had the first contact with editing web pages. I've made the website of the foundation. Fortunately, these kinds of foundations were no longer needed, but a fragment from their old site can still be found here.
