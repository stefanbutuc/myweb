1.1 Describing the purpose of IT in modern business

The Internet and IT begin to occupy an increasingly important place in the modern world. The IT domain has far outstripped the level of entertainment, information, communication. Now we can say that it became the backbone of any other kind of human activity: from medicine to programming industrial robots, from kitchen appliances to quantum research. In other words, no human activity can be conceived outside IT. “There is no life beyond IT”!!! I totally disapprove with this idea, from a moral and cultural point of view, but it reality can not be denied. Technologies have overcrowded our lives. We can only adapt to them and rethink the place of technology in our lives.

However, there are many positive effects of IT in our lives and we must insist on them.

Uni Assignment Centre (www.uniassignment.com) assert as “Today, technology is becoming very important in our daily lives. it affects individuals, communities, businesses and the nation. highly technological impact in the business world. it has helped in terms of management, manufacturing, marketing of communication products and modular type more easily.” They found the role of Information Technology in Business in many fields: Communication, Inventory Management (Storing and Protecting Information), Management Information Systems, Customer Relationship Management, Working Away.
