1.7 Describe legal guidelines and constraints that impact digital projects – How does GDPR impact digital projects?

The most important law that constrains IT activity is the right to intellectual property (copyright).

A second important law is GDPR (“The General Data Protection Regulation”). Simply put, the GDPR mandates a baseline set of standards for companies that handle EU citizens’ data to better safeguard the processing and movement of citizens’ personal data.

Depending on the field in which IT is involved, there are many other laws to guide it. But these two above all have general applicability.
