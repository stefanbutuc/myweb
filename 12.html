Why I chose Code Nation

Code Nation is much more than just another company that teaches coding. They offer a complete education in digital technologies, that focuses on skills like cyber security, data analysis and AI, because they’re the most in-demand skills in every industry and in every company, be it smaller or larger.

I was impressed by the high employment rate of the graduates of this school (over 90% of them found a job in less than six days after graduation).

But what I liked most was the working method: coding bootcamp! There is not only an online course, but a direct contact with teachers and colleagues. The support is direct, face to face, not just abstract. The atmosphere is very open and the collaboration very tight. This provides and ensures a very good understanding and learning of information and knowledge. The learning process takes place in terms of effective work, projects and assessments. Teachers are very dedicated and adapting their teaching process to the profile and level of training and learning of each student.

For these reasons, not only I strongly recommend to anyone who wants to start a coding career to choose Code Nation, but I sincerely believe that any employer can find a very qualified and competitive employee among Code Nation graduates.
